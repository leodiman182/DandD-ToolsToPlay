About Us

Welcome to Dungeons & Dragons - Tools to Play! The idea of this project is to ease and connect even more D&D players, providing a practical tool for creating, sharing and saving your created characters. We provide a really simple form to help you build the character you are creating in the best way possible.

Also, we provide access to the complete library of the D&D5 universe and - believe it or not - digital dices for you to roll! Basically, we give you everything you need to join the infinite and amazing universe of Dungeons and Dragons.
